About Me

I am a passionate Full Stack Developer with a unique blend of skills stemming from both marketing and full-stack web development backgrounds. After earning my BA in Marketing, with a minor in Applied Computing from the University of Washington, I was intrigued by the synergy between technology and marketing. This drove me to complete two bootcamps at Nucamp, graduating with honors in full stack and mobile development.

My hands-on experience spans across various facets of web development, with a special focus on Shopify, Node.js, and React. Through me work experience, I've honed my expertise in delivering efficient solutions, balancing technical needs with optimal user experiences. My work in these roles encompassed diverse tasks, from web development and analytics optimization to SEO and inventory system integrations.

Beyond technical prowess, I pride myself on my adaptability, drive, and collaborative spirit. I'm a firm believer in the power of teamwork and am continually on the lookout for opportunities to learn and stay abreast with the dynamic tech landscape. I'm not just seeking to stand out among developers; I aspire to merge my diverse skills to bring unparalleled value to any team I join.

Node Warranty App

A full stack application developed to handle warranties. Client is able to make API calls to register a product, view registered product for specific customers, and change status of a warranty. In order to validate registration, serial numbers are moved from a "serial numbers" collection to the "registered products collection" upon registration. The admin panel (pictured) gives authenticated users the ability to see all warranties and change their status from "active" to "inactive". Technologies used include Koa, React, Next.js, and the Fetch API. This app is currently running on Heroku.
